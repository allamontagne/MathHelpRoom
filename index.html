Welcome to the Math Help Room

What We Do

Located on the first floor of Pfahler Hall of Science, the Math Help Room is an open space for students to work together or get help from student tutors in the major. More than ten tutors, specialising in Statistics, Calculus, Theoretical Math, and Computer Science, hold open office hours every week where students can drop in to get help with homework, labs, or review concepts from class.

The Math Help Room is found in Pfahler 102 and is always open for students. For specific tutoring hours, click here.
